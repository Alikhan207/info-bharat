National Scheme Performance

Where central schemes are reaching intended beneficiaries and where policy design needs revision.

🌾 PM-KISAN Design Flaw Detected

Land record revision cycles in 6 states cause February-March rejection spikes annually. This is a structural policy issue — not implementation failure. 280,000 eligible farmers affected each cycle.
Policy RecommendationIntroduce a 45-day eligibility bridge period during state land record revisions. Estimated annual beneficiary protection: 280,000 farmers.

🏥 Ayushman Last-Mile Gap

39% of BPL households in tribal districts cannot access Ayushman due to absence of empanelled hospitals within 50km. Scheme design assumes hospital proximity that doesn't exist in these geographies.
Policy RecommendationExpand mobile health unit empanelment. Pilot reimbursement at primary health centres for tribal districts in 4 states.
